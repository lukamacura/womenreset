  Modern Menopausal Hormone Therapy (MHT) offers safer delivery methods and bioidentical formulations compared to options from 20 years ago. 

  We now use bioidentical hormones ‚Äî molecules chemically identical to those produced by your body ‚Äî delivered through methods that reduce side effects and risk.
  
  **Transdermal estrogen (patches, gels, or sprays)** delivers hormones directly through the skin, avoiding first-pass metabolism in the liver. This lowers the risk of blood clots and stroke compared to oral tablets.
  
  **Micronized progesterone** is a body-identical form that's better tolerated than older synthetic progestins and may even support sleep.
  
  The key is personalization: choosing the right delivery method for your unique health profile, lifestyle, and preferences.
